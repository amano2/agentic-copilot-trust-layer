POLICY DOCUMENT: THEFT AND BURGLARY CLAUSES (SEC-103)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicability: Homeowners, Renters, and Personal Property Policies

1. COVERED LOSSES
- Loss of personal property owned or used by an insured due to theft, burglary, or robbery, while located at the insured premises or temporarily off-premises (e.g., in a hotel or vehicle, subject to limits).

2. MANDATORY EVIDENCE AND CONDITIONS
- The insured must report the theft to the police department or local law enforcement authorities within 24 hours of discovering the theft.
- A copy of the official police report, including a case reference number, MUST be submitted alongside the insurance claim.
- Claims filed without an official police report, or filed more than 7 days after the occurrence of the theft, will be denied automatically.

3. LIMITATIONS ON VALUABLES
- Coverage for jewelry, watches, furs, precious stones, and electronics is limited to a maximum of $1,500 per single occurrence, unless the items have been individually appraised and listed under a Personal Property Scheduling Rider.
