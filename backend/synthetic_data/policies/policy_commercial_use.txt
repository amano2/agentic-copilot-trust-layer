POLICY DOCUMENT: COMMERCIAL VEHICLE AND RIDESHARING EXCLUSIONS (SEC-105)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicability: Personal Automobile Insurance Policies

1. COMMERCIAL USE EXCLUSION
This insurance policy is issued for personal, recreational, and standard commuting use of the insured vehicle only. No coverage is provided under this policy for any loss, damage, or liability that occurs while the vehicle is being used for commercial purposes.

2. DEFINITIONS OF COMMERCIAL USE
Commercial purposes include, but are not limited to:
- Carrying passengers for a fee, fee-on-board, or fare, including ridesharing services (e.g., Uber, Lyft) while the app is active or a passenger is in the vehicle.
- Delivering food, goods, packages, or materials for a fee (e.g., DoorDash, UberEats, Amazon Flex, courier services).
- Use of the vehicle by an employee or independent contractor for business tasks, sales calls, or transporting equipment.

3. RIDER REQUIREMENT
Insureds who engage in ridesharing or commercial delivery must purchase a Commercial Use Rider or a rideshare-specific policy endorsement. Standard personal auto policy coverages are completely suspended during periods of commercial activity.
