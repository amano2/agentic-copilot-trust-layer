POLICY DOCUMENT: WATER DAMAGE AND PLUMBING CLAUSES (SEC-102)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicability: All Homeowners and Property Policies

1. COVERED LOSSES
- Sudden and accidental discharge or overflow of water or steam from within a plumbing, heating, air conditioning, or household appliance.
- Cost of tearing out and replacing any part of the building necessary to repair the system or appliance from which the water or steam escaped.

2. SPECIFIC EXCLUSIONS (GRADUAL LEAKS)
- The Company does not cover loss or damage caused by constant, repeated, or gradual leakage, seepage, or condensation of water or steam over a period of weeks, months, or years.
- Insureds are expected to maintain the property in good repair. Any leak that shows evidence of existing for more than 14 days prior to detection (e.g., extensive mold, rotting wood, rusted pipes) will be deemed a maintenance issue and is excluded from coverage.
- Water damage caused by backup of sewers or drains, or water overflowing from a sump pump, is excluded unless a separate Sewer Backup Rider has been purchased.
