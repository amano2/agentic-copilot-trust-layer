POLICY DOCUMENT: GENERAL POLICY EXCLUSIONS (SEC-108)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicability: All Personal Lines Insurance Policies

1. WEAR AND TEAR / MAINTENANCE EXCLUSION
This policy does not cover any loss, damage, or expense caused directly or indirectly by:
- Wear and tear, gradual deterioration, rust, corrosion, wet or dry rot.
- Mechanical breakdown, electrical failure, or structural settling.
- Insureds have a duty to perform routine maintenance on their property.

2. CRIMINAL AND ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES
No coverage is provided for any loss or liability arising out of:
- Any illegal act, criminal enterprise, or transport of contraband by an insured.
- Operating any vehicle while under the influence of alcohol (BAC > 0.08) or narcotics, in violation of state law.

3. ARSON AND INTENTIONAL DAMAGE
Any damage caused intentionally by an insured or at the direction of an insured is strictly excluded. Such claims will be denied, and details will be forwarded to the National Insurance Crime Bureau (NICB) for fraud investigation.
