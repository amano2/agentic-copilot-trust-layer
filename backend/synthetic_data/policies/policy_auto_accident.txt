POLICY DOCUMENT: AUTO ACCIDENT COVERAGE (SEC-101)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applicability: All Personal Automobile Insurance Policies

1. COVERAGE STATEMENT
The Company will pay for sudden, direct, and accidental loss or damage to the insured vehicle resulting from collision with another vehicle or object, or upset/rollover of the vehicle, subject to the exclusions and deductibles listed herein.

2. REQUIREMENT FOR REPORTING
- Insureds must report all auto accidents to the police department within 24 hours of the occurrence if the accident involves bodily injury or property damage exceeding $1,000.
- Failure to obtain a police report for major accidents (estimated damage > $2,500) may result in claims being delayed or subject to strict verification.

3. EXCLUSIONS
This coverage does not apply to:
- Any loss occurring while the vehicle is operated by an unlicensed driver or a driver whose license is currently suspended.
- Any loss occurring while the vehicle is participating in a race, speed contest, or stunt driving.
- Any damage occurring when the vehicle is driven off-road or in non-designated transit lanes, unless under emergency direction.
- Wear and tear, mechanical breakdown, electrical failure, or road damage to tires (unless resulting from collision).
